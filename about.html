About The Getaway School

Founded in 1985, The Getaway School has been dedicated to providing quality education and fostering intellectual growth for over three decades. With 10 campuses across the country, we are proud to be a leader in academic excellence.

Our mission is to empower students with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in a rapidly changing world.

We offer a wide range of academic programs and extracurricular activities that cater to the diverse needs of our students.
